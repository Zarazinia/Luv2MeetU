Welcome to our interactive conversation help page! It is designed to help you with conversation with your friends, meeting new people or on a date.

You can recap and pick up ideas to give you confidence before -hand or use it together with your friends, taking it in turns to press the button and ask the questions.

You’ll also find tips on conversation, social situations and how to be successful at communicating. This will make every-one happy!

Here’s how it works: simply choose the category of questions that you would like to ask to your friends and press the button to see what question comes up and ask away! Let your friends or your date have a go and ask the next question.
