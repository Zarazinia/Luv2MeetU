Some dating tips to think about

Conversation Tips

Keep your conversation short but don’t give one word answers.

Make sure you listen to the other person and ask them questions about what they’ve told you.

Make sure your tone of voice is friendly.

Don’t interrupt the other person, this can be seen as very rude!

If conversation gets stuck use the questions to get it started again, they’ll be things to ask to break the ice or get the conversation flowing again.

Make sure you let the other people/person speak.

Another way to get a conversation started is to ask the other person to tell a story about something funny that’s happened to them or trip abroad. If you are in a group you could all tell your own story, see who has the funniest, happiest or most scary!

Be kind and thoughtful when hearing what others have to say. Don’t put some-one or their ideas down just because you don’t agree with them.

You can disagree with some-one and stay friendly!

Dating Tips

By smiling at your date, it shows that you are happy to be with them. If you are sat at a table together, it's good to face them to show that you are interested in talking to them.

You may want to give your date a hug when you first see them and they might want to hug you too. However, not everybody likes to be hugged on a first date. It might be a good idea to ask your date if they would like a hug before doing so, and if they say no, this is absolutely fine.

Ask your date questions to get to know them, but also to make sure that you talk about what your likes and dislikes are too, so that you can both get to know each other.

Remember that if you don't think that you have romantic feelings for your date then this is absolutely fine. It is very normal to go on a first date but feel like you don't want to have a relationship with this person. Many people go on lots of first dates before finding a relationship.
